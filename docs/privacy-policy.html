Privacy Policy

Quick Filter for Online Sellers (TikTok + Shopee)

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

All data stored by this extension (product aliases, print history, planning sessions) is kept exclusively in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB on your local device. No data ever leaves your browser.

Network Requests

This extension only makes network requests to TikTok Seller Centre (seller-th.tiktok.com) and Shopee Seller Centre (seller.shopee.co.th) — the same domains you are already logged into as a seller. No data is sent to any other server.

Permissions

The extension requires host permissions for seller-th.tiktok.com and seller.shopee.co.th solely to inject the floating widget interface and intercept shipping label API responses for display purposes.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/Sittipanpee/tiktok-quick-filter.
